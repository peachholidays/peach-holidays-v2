# Homepage High-Fidelity Wireframe: WebAURA & WebFUNNEL

**Objective**: Maximize Marketing ROI via "Least-Click-Arrival" UX.

## Section 1: Hero (The Arrival)
- **Visual Strategy**: Slow-motion cinematic video loop (Atmospheric travel moments) or dynamic image layer. 
- **Component**: **Mega-Search Bar Overlay**.
    - *Fields*: Destination, Energy Type (Vibe), Dates.
    - *Conversion Trigger*: Immediate "Discover" button leading to `/tours`.

## Section 2: Horizontal Scroll 1 (Upcoming Trips)
- **Visuals**: Large, high-resolution immersive cards.
- **Data Placeholders**:
    - `Destination Name` (H3)
    - `Starting Price` (Dynamic based on Tier)
    - `Dates & Inventory` (Real-time Firestore feedback)
- **Action**: Sticky "Book Now" CTA on card hover.

## Section 3: Horizontal Scroll 2 (What's Hot)
- **Visuals**: Focus on "Vibe" and "Aura" photography rather than dates.
- **Differentiator**: Taller card format with "DNA Radar" visualization (Adrenaline vs. Serenity).
- **Trigger**: "Heart" save icon for user-profile bookmarks.

## Section 4: Static Service Sections
- **'Our Services'**: 3-column grid (Fixed Departures, Insurance, Custom Tours).
- **'Why Peach?'**: High-contrast typography grid highlighting "Expert Curation" and "AI-Driven Insights".
- **'Testimonials'**: Glassmorphic carousel featuring social proof with Millennial/Gen Z user avatars.

## Section 5: Conversion Anchor (The Closer)
- **Newsletter**: Minimalist glassmorphic form. 
- **Sticky CTA**: "Book Your Energy" button anchored to the bottom-right for thumb-reach optimization on mobile devices.
